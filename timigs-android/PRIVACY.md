# Privacy Policy for TimiGS Android

**Last Updated**: January 28, 2026

## Overview

TimiGS Android ("the App") is an activity tracking application that helps users monitor their app usage and productivity. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

The App collects the following data **locally on your device**:

1. **App Usage Data**
   - Application names you use
   - Time spent in each application
   - Session start and end times
   - Window titles (when available)

2. **User Preferences**
   - Language preference
   - Theme preference (Dark/Light)
   - Tracking status (On/Off)

3. **Tasks and Goals**
   - App usage goals you create
   - Task descriptions and progress

### What We DON'T Collect

- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Contacts
- Photos or media
- Any data from other apps
- Browsing history
- Passwords or credentials

## Data Storage

### Local Storage

**All data is stored locally on your device** using SQLite database. We do NOT send your data to any external servers.

- Database location: App's private storage
- Data is NOT accessible to other apps
- Data is deleted when you uninstall the app

### Cloud Backup (Optional)

If you choose to enable Google Drive sync (future feature):
- Data is encrypted before upload
- Only you can access your data via your Google account
- You can delete cloud backups at any time
- This feature is entirely optional

## Data Usage

We use the collected data solely for:

1. Displaying your app usage statistics
2. Generating charts and analytics
3. Tracking progress toward your goals
4. Providing historical timeline view

## Data Sharing

**We do NOT share your data with anyone.** Period.

- No third-party analytics
- No advertising networks
- No data brokers
- No external servers (except optional Google Drive backup)

## Permissions

### Required Permissions

1. **Usage Stats (PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS)**
   - Purpose: Track which apps you use and for how long
   - This is the core functionality of the app
   - Must be granted manually in Android Settings

### Optional Permissions

2. **Internet**
   - Purpose: Future Google Drive sync feature
   - Currently not used

3. **Foreground Service**
   - Purpose: Keep tracking active in background
   - Ensures accurate time tracking

4. **Notifications**
   - Purpose: Show tracking status
   - Can be disabled in Android settings

5. **Wake Lock**
   - Purpose: Prevent tracking interruption
   - Minimal battery impact

6. **Boot Completed**
   - Purpose: Auto-start tracking after device restart
   - Only if you had tracking enabled

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

1. **Access Your Data**: All data is visible in the app
2. **Export Your Data**: Use the "Export Database" feature in Settings
3. **Delete Your Data**: Uninstall the app or clear app data in Android Settings
4. **Disable Tracking**: Toggle tracking OFF in Settings at any time

## Data Security

We implement security measures to protect your data:

- Local database is in app's private storage
- No network transmission (except optional Google Drive)
- No cloud storage by default
- Data encrypted when using Google Drive backup

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you are a parent and believe your child has used this app, please uninstall it from their device.
