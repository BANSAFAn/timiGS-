# Welcome to TimiGS

**TimiGS** is the ultimate desktop utility for productivity and system management.

## Why TimiGS?

- **Modern UI**: Sleek, glass-like interface.
- **Privacy First**: Your data stays on your device (or your Cloud).
- **Extensible**: Built with web technologies.

> [!TIP]
> Use the sidebar to navigate topics.

## Quick Start

1. Download the installer.
2. Run `TimiGS Setup.exe`.
3. Enjoy!
